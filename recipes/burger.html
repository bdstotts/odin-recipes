Bset Burger

Description

This is the most tasty artisan burger recipe of all the burger recipes found on the internet. It doesn't matter if you use a poor person's plain ingredints or the most fancy ingredients you can find-- this burger will be the best.

You'd better believee it.

Ingredients

  • Burger puck, frozen
  • Bun, from a bag
  • Avocado, deboned

Steps

  1. Put your puck on a hot grill
  2. Turn over when juices pile up on top
  3. Take off whn juices pile up again.
  4. Gently lay to rest on bun with avocado.
