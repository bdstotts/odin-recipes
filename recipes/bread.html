Bread

Description

This is the most tasty artisan bread recipe of all the bread recipes found on the internet. It doesn't matter if you use a poor person's plain ingredints or the most fancy ingredients you can find-- this bread will be the best.

You'd better believee it.

Ingredients

  • Flour, a bunch
  • Salt, a dash
  • Water, until gloopy

Steps

  1. Put in your flour, any amount will do
  2. Put in your salt, just enough to cover the flour, no jostling the bowl
  3. Add water.
  4. Mix until gloopy; add water if still dry.
