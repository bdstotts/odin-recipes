Spicy Lime Marinade

Description

This is the most tasty marniade recipe of all the marinade recipes found on the internet. It doesn't matter if you use a poor person's plain ingredints or the most fancy ingredients you can find-- this marinade will be the best.

You'd better believee it.

Ingredients

  • lime, a pair
  • Serranos, a handful
  • liquids

Steps

  1. Put in your limes' juice and zest, any amount will do
  2. Put in your serranos, just enough to cover the lime, no jostling the bowl
  3. Add liquids.
  4. Stir until gloopy; add liquids if still dry.
